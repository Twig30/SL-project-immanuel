What is the Amazon Rainforest? The Amazon Rainforest is a forest that is located in South America, and spans in 9 different countries, some of these countries are Brazil,Peru,Bolivia,and Ecuador etc. The Amazon Rainforest is home to over 30 billion trees and 10,000 different animal species, which is why it is super important to preserve this forestfrom many different environmental issues such as deforestation and environment loss.
